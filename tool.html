Missing articles by occupation and country

This tool queries Wikidata in real time to find women who have a Wikidata item but no English Wikipedia article. Filter by occupation, by country, or by both at once.

Results match the format used on the Women in Red Redlist Index — each row shows the woman's name (as a red link for article creation), description, occupation (P106), country of citizenship (P27), date of birth (P569), date of death (P570), Wikidata item ID, and sitelink count.

Select an occupation and/or a country above, then click Search Wikidata.
